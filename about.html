ABOUT STEGANO.IO

Stegano.io is a project designed to encode and decode hidden messages in images using the Least Significant Bit (LSB) algorithm. This technique allows users to securely embed confidential information within images, making it invisible to the naked eye.

The purpose of this project is to enhance digital privacy and secure communication by integrating steganography into everyday use. Whether you need to hide sensitive data or send secret messages, Stegano.io provides a simple and effective solution.

🔒 Why Choose Stegano.io?

  • ✅ Advanced LSB Encoding - Ensures high-level security for hidden messages.
  • ✅ User-Friendly Interface - Simple and easy-to-use design.
  • ✅ Fast & Secure - Quick encoding and decoding process without data loss.
  • ✅ Free to Use - No hidden costs or subscriptions.
